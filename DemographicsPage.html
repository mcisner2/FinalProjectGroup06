    Demographics of Housing in Texas and California

    The demographics subset of the project will present visualizations of demographics of household owners within California and Texas. These demographics include race, age, and education background.

    Sunburst Chart of Educational Background of Homeowners in Texas and California

    The following visualizations present a sunburst chart of the educational background that household owners of Texas and California have:

    Insights

    From the visualizations above, we are able to make the following insights:

    • California has more homeowners with a bachelors degree or higher than Texas does.
    • California has more homeowners with a college or associate's degree than Texas does.
    • California has more homeowners with less than a high school degree than Texas does.
    • Texas has more homeowners with a high school degree than California does.
    • California overall has more homeowners who have above a high school degree than Texas does.

    Histograms of the Races of Owners and Renters of Households in Texas and California

    The following visualizations present histograms for both Texas and California and the races of their household owners and renters:

    Insights

    From the visualizations above, we are able to make the following insights:

    • The largest amount of total homes in renters/owners for both state's races was White. In the majority of cases more people owned than rented their home for both states.
    • In both states, Asians had the largest ratio between owners and renters.
    • In the majority of cases more people owned than rented their home for both states.
    • In Texas, a larger number of African Americans made up homes compared to California, but the opposite is true for Asians. California had a greater number of total amount of homes owned/rented by all races compared to Texas.
    • In California, some other race made up the second largest amount of total homes while in Texas it was African Americans.

    Insights

    From the visualizations above, we are able to make the following insights:

    Insights

    From the visualizations above, we are able to make the following insights:
